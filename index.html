My name is Jaime Mitchell

Hey there! This page marks the beginning of my 15 week journey into a promising career as a software engineer!

Here's how it began for me:

I was a gardener and arborist by trade until both a previous disability and a back injury prevented me from the work I really dug (no pun intended).

I moved into my car and tried a previous side hustle as an event photographer and cinematographer, but gigs were seldom and pay wasn't great, then the pandemic hit.

However, I had a ton going for me as well. I had a wealth of time and the isolation made me focus my mind on other career paths.

I picked up a cheap Raspberry Pi and a knock-off Arduino kit (Elegoo) and taught myself a little bit about microelectronics, embedded coding and the Command Line.

With every successful program written I got some confidence back and eventually applied to this program.
